About us
etiki brings transparency to the business world

We create a platform that will help you to inform yourself about the ethical and sustainable behaviour of companies.

About current and past events. With which other companies a specific company is connected (suppliers, owners, subsidiaries, etc.). All information reliable, easy to filter and free of charge! Sounds good? Yes, we are not quite there yet.

Well, what you will find on the prototype

On prealpha.etiki you can search for news articles about ethical and sustainable behaviour of companies. You can either search for the company or for a category that interests you. This helps you to form your opinion about the companies so that you can use it for future purchase decisions.

Have fun informing about ethics and sustainability!
